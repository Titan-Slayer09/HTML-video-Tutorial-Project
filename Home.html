Here is How to play chess

  • 1. Learn how the pieces move, use the video to learn if needed.
  • 2. Study chess theory, learn openings, and ways to checkmate, such as the Scholar's Mate which you can learn from the video above.
  • 3. Chess requires an opponent, use chess.com or make friends (probably for your first time)
  • 4. Play a game, win maybe, and become a grandmaster

    piece movement importance
    Pawn Moves forward two on his first move, the one for the rest of the game. worth 1 point
    Knight Moves in L shapes, can jump over other pieces worth 3 points
    Bishop moves diagnonally Worth 3 points
    Rook moves in straight lines worth 5 points
    Queen moves like every other piece except the Knight worth 7 points
    King moves one space in any direction worth everything, defend at all costs
